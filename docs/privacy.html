Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-05

This policy describes how Ujani Herbals WhatsApp Assistant (“the Service”) processes information.

What we collect

  • Messages you send to our WhatsApp number (text, buttons, media) so we can respond and fulfil your requests.
  • Order and delivery details you provide (name, phone, area/district, address notes).
  • Payment confirmations you share (e.g., reference text or screenshots).

How we use it

  • To reply to you on WhatsApp and operate the shopping experience.
  • To create and manage orders, delivery, and support.
  • To prevent fraud and keep the Service secure.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited information with service providers (e.g., WhatsApp/Meta, payment and delivery partners) strictly to operate the Service.

Data retention

Order records are retained as required for operations, accounting, and legal obligations. Chat logs may be kept for support and abuse prevention, then deleted or anonymized.

Your choices

  • You may ask us to delete your data (see instructions below).
  • You may stop using the Service and block our WhatsApp number at any time.
